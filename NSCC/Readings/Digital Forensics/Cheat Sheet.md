### **2. THE ETHICS HIERARCHY (ISC2)**

*Memorize this specific order. If #1 and #3 conflict, #1 wins.*

1. **PROTECT SOCIETY** (Public safety, infrastructure, common good).
* *Red Flag:* Life or death, public danger.


2. **ACT HONORABLY** (Honesty, justice, law).
* *Red Flag:* Lying, stealing, hacking back.


3. **PROVIDE DILIGENT SERVICE** (Duty to client/boss).
* *Red Flag:* Doing a bad job, ignoring instructions (unless they break #1 or #2).


4. **PROTECT THE PROFESSION** (Reputation).
* *Red Flag:* Helping criminals, being unprofessional.



---

### **3. THE CHAIN OF CUSTODY (The "Receipt")**

*If the chain breaks, the case breaks.*

* **The Golden Rule:** There can be **NO GAPS** in the timeline.
* *Bad:* Released 10:00 AM -> Received 2:00 PM. (Where was it for 4 hours?)
* *Good:* Released 10:00 AM -> Received 10:00 AM.


* **Signatures:** The person giving it and the person taking it must sign **at the same time**.
* **Identifiers:** You must list the **Serial Number**. (Description alone like "Black Dell Laptop" is not enough).
