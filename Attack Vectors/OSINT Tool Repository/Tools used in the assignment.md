OSRFramework
=============
OSRFramework is a collection of scripts for OSINT tasks, including username checking, DNS lookups, and gathering information from various sources.

Checkfy (OSRFramework) --> Checkfy verifies the existence of email accounts across various providers

Searchfy (OSRFramework) --> Searchfy searches for profiles or information about a target across various platforms. Part of the OSRFramework, it aids in comprehensive data gathering.

Alias Generator (OSRFramework) --> Alias Generator creates possible usernames or aliases based on input data. This helps expand search parameters when looking for online profiles.

Infoga --> an email OSINT tool for gathering information about email accounts using public data source

Mailfy (OSRFramework) --> helps find email addresses related to a domain or person by searching through different databases and services.

Usufy (OSRFramework) --> Usufy checks for the existence of a given username across numerous platforms, assisting in mapping a person's online presence.
