


### Network Scanning with Nessus 


#### Prepwork 


1. Set **UAC** to *0* on the **Target Machine**
2. Allow all *TCP Ports* and *ICMP* on the **Target Machine** and **Our Machine**.

	1. Login as an *Administrator* to change the **Firewall**
	2. Use the **Builtin Local Administrator Account's Credentials** to do the *Privileged Scan*.
